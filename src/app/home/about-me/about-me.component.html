About me

My Studies

All my knowledge comes from my "self-taught" studies and the experience acquired in my projects.

From time to time, I like to put together a study plan based on the things that I feel like learning and think will be most useful to me. Then I dedicate a couple of hours each day until I complete it.

My daily life

I consider it extremely important to maintain a balanced life, which implies:

  • Not to exceed my working hours.
  • Doing sports several times a week.
  • Every so often while using the computer, go for a walk to clear my mind.
  • Leave some time to disconnect from everything and enjoy life.
